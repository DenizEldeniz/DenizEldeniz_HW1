Games I Play & Recommend

  • League of Legends (LoL)
    I've been playing for 9 years and I still can't stop! A team-based strategy and action-packed MOBA game. My friend Arda and I have been playing together every evening since high school. Climbing ranked together is a whole different kind of fun. If you enjoy competitive games and value teamwork, this game is perfect for you!
  • Valorant
    I've been playing for 3 years and I absolutely love it! An exciting tactical FPS that combines gunplay with character abilities. My friend Burak and I love queuing up as a 5-stack premade for competitive matches. We literally live for those clutch moments! If you like CS-style games but want different mechanics, you should definitely try Valorant!
  • Albion Online (Steam)
    I've been playing for 2 years. An open-world sandbox MMORPG full of economy, PvP, and crafting systems. Creating a guild with my friend Emre and doing PvP in the black zone gives us adrenaline-filled moments. If you're looking for a free world where you can write your own story, definitely try Albion Online!
  • Minecraft
    I've been playing for 15 years — yes, really since childhood. The legendary sandbox game offering limitless creativity and adventure. I'll never forget the days we built massive castles in survival worlds. Build alone or survive with friends — your imagination is the limit!
  • DEVOUR
    I've only been playing for 1 year but it's already one of my favorite horror games! A 1-4 player co-op horror game. Last year my friend Mert and I started playing at 3 AM, we got so scared that we shut down the computer and went to sleep. If you love horror games and want to experience thrilling moments with friends, DEVOUR is perfect for you!
